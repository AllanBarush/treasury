Program Structure

Twelve comprehensive modules covering the full spectrum of treasury and financial operations.

Module 1: Treasury Fundamentals

Core treasury functions, organizational structures, and governance frameworks.

Module 2: Cash Management

Cash positioning, pooling structures, and payment/collection optimization.

Module 3: Liquidity Planning

Short and long-term liquidity forecasting and stress testing methodologies.

Module 4: FX Operations

Foreign exchange markets, hedging strategies, and multi-currency management.

Module 5: Interest Rate Management

Yield curve analysis, interest rate derivatives, and ALM principles.

Module 6: Trade Finance

Letters of credit, supply chain finance, and international trade instruments.

Module 7: Bank Relationship Management

Bank scoring, RFP processes, and multi-bank technology platforms.

Module 8: Treasury Technology

TMS selection, implementation, and integration with ERP systems.

Module 9: Regulatory & Compliance

IFRS, SOX, Dodd-Frank, and EMIR implications for treasury operations.

Module 10: Investment Management

Short-term investment policies, money market instruments, and portfolio management.

Module 11: Digital Treasury

APIs, real-time payments, virtual accounts, and blockchain applications.

Module 12: Strategic Treasury

M&A treasury integration, capital structure optimization, and board-level reporting.
